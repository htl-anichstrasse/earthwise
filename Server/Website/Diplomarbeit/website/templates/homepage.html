Earthwise

Earthwise is a project that aims to bring geographical knowledge closer to people around the world. In our globalizing world, we increasingly hear about events occurring worldwide. However, most people struggle to associate the mentioned countries with a geographical location or recognize their respective flags.

Earthwise strives to close this knowledge gap and provide people of all ages with a deeper understanding of geography. In an era of globalization where information from around the world is easily accessible, it is crucial that people not only hear about events but also have the ability to understand the geographical contexts.

Through interactive and innovative learning methods, Earthwise seeks to overcome this challenge. Many individuals face difficulties in locating countries on a world map or identifying their flags. Our goal is to enhance these skills in a playful and engaging manner.
